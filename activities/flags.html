The National Flag of India (Hindi: Tiraṅgā) is a horizontal rectangular tricolour of India saffron, white and India green; with the Ashoka Chakra, a 24-spoke wheel, in navy blue at its centre.

The National Flag of Paraguay is a red–white–blue triband, was inspired by the colours of the French Tricolour, believed to signify independence and liberty.
The flag is unusual because it differs on its obverse and reverse sides:
the obverse of the flag shows the national coat of arms, and the reverse shows the seal of the treasury.

Instructions

This is going to be your final activity for this year. Your work should look exactly like the screenshot attached. NO MORE, NO LESS. Please be reminded that the flag you see is not entirely an image, so you can't just download a flag image of India and Paraguay and put it inside the <img> element. The only image that you are allowed to use is the logo of each of these countries' national flags which is apparently in the middle, inside the white part of the flags. I will be attaching the logos of each of these flags for you to use. As for the colors, make sure, that you use regular HTML colors, RGB, HEX, HSL values because I will be checking it in your codes. You may use a combination of elements like for e.g (table elements, div, span, and other elements that you think are deemed appropriate in making the colored bars of each of the flags). On the footer part, use text elements that make use of semantic tags.
